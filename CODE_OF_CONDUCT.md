# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community. Contribution and collaboration should remain professional and inclusive.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Use professional language.
- Value constructive feedback.
- Report harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discriminatory remarks.
- Abusive or demeaning communication.
- Posting content that undermines contributors or the project.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, raise an issue or contact the maintainers.
