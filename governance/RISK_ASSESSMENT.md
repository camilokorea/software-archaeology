# Risk Assessment

## Objective
Assess modernization risk across technical, operational, and business dimensions.

## Scope
This artifact covers risk assessment.md.

## Instructions
Document risk factors, likelihood, impact, and mitigation strategies.

## Expected Outputs
A risk assessment artifact that supports decision-making and prioritization.

## Example Outputs
A risk register with clearly defined risk categories and recommended controls.

## Analysis Rules
Use factual evidence and avoid speculative risk statements.

## Suggested Extensions
Extend with risk heat maps and scenario planning.
